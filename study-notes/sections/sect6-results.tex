\subsection*{\Cref{sect:int-pt-methods}}
\item \Cref{lma:form-ellip-interior}: preliminary result for forming ellipsoids in interior
\item \Cref{prp:min-ellip-closed-form}: closed-form formula for optimal
solution \(\vect{d}^{*}\) to the problem of minimization over ellipsoid
\item \Cref{prp:near-primal-dual-optimal}: optimal condition for ``near
optimality'' for primal and dual
\item \Cref{prp:max-norm-movement-interior}: long-step variant with maximum
norm (and also \(\gamma\)) leads to movements within interior
\item \Cref{thm:affine-scal-conv}: convergence of affine scaling algorithm
\item \Cref{prp:affine-scal-big-m}: justification for the ``big-\(M\) method'' for initializing the affine scaling algorithm
\item \Cref{lma:poten-fn-lb}: lower bound on the potential function
\item \Cref{thm:poten-redu-iter-num}: number of iterations for getting \(\varepsilon\)-optimal solutions by potential reduction
